Building UDB Compliance is Complex

The EU Union Database requires economic operators to submit and maintain product information through standardized digital channels. For businesses without dedicated IT systems, this presents significant technical challenges.

The AS4 messaging standard and eDelivery protocol demand specialized infrastructure that most operators simply cannot build or maintain cost-effectively.

  • Technical Complexity AS4 implementation requires deep expertise in eDelivery protocols and EU digital infrastructure
  • Infrastructure Costs Building and maintaining compliant access points demands significant IT investment
  • Ongoing Updates Regulatory changes require continuous system updates and monitoring
  • Time to Compliance In-house development delays your ability to meet regulatory deadlines
